# About Me

I am a Software Engineer at **TikTok (ByteDance)**, working on the Growth & CRM Platform team in San Jose, CA. I build scalable systems that power lead acquisition, marketing automation, and AI-driven sales intelligence at scale.

I hold a **Master of Computer Science** from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign and a **Bachelor of Computer Science** (Highest Distinction, 4.0 GPA) from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor.

## What I Do

At TikTok, I focus on building full-lifecycle growth platforms that unify data from multiple sources, enable personalized marketing campaigns, and leverage LLM-powered agents for intelligent lead research. My work spans backend systems in **Java** and **Go**, data pipelines with **Spark**, and AI agent architectures using the **Eino framework**.

Previously at **Hughes Network Systems**, I developed critical gateway software for in-flight connectivity serving Delta and Spirit Airlines, working with **C++**, **Kafka**, and distributed systems infrastructure.

Outside of work, I enjoy traveling and experiencing different cultures. I'm passionate about building systems that solve real-world problems at scale, and I'm always exploring new technologies in the AI/ML and distributed systems space.
